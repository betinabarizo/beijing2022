Germany's golden duo set new benchmark in figure skating pairs

Germans Aljona Savchenko and Bruno MAssot set a pairs free programme world record of 159.31 points after a stunning routine at Pyeonchang 2018 on 15 february to win the pairs figure skating gold. The pair collapsed and cried on the ice after a performance t hat saw them beat the previous record that they had also set at the Grand Prix Final last year by more than two points.

Aljona Savchenko's guide at figure skating

“I feel free on the ice, I love the atmosphere,” she said. “It’s hard to explain, but when you go to a competition you are going to work, but you are doing what you love. I enjoy every second of it, the adrenaline flowing, the public giving you energy and you giving it back.”
